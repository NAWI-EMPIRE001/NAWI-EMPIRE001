PRIVACY POLICY FOR NAWI-EMPIRE

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

1. Our Core Privacy Philosophy: Zero-Trust

At NAWI-EMPIRE, we operate under a strict Zero-Trust architectural framework. We believe that your digital footprint and your funds belong to you alone. We do not harvest data for profit or build behavioral user tracking profiles for external corporate marketing.

Most importantly: We do not collect your data to hand over to public services, government agencies, or corporate third parties. All operational information remains strictly isolated, encrypted, and protected within the secure boundaries of the NAWI-EMPIRE ecosystem.

2. The "Level-Up" Verification Data Structure

To ensure a smooth onboarding experience while maintaining elite ecosystem security, NAWI-EMPIRE collects and maintains information in distinct structural tiers based on your platform activity, tenure, and challenge registration:

📈 Tier 1: The Casual Citizen (Day 1 Activation)

To unlock basic task execution across the 7 Pillars while preventing account hijacking and automated malicious network nodes, the system logs:

  • Registration Anchors: Your primary email address and primary phone number. These are permanently bound to your profile and serve as the exclusive channels for the dual-channel recovery protocol.
  • System Verification Video: A secure, encrypted 10-second front-facing video recorded on Day 1. This serves as your biological signature, completely locked away from public view, used strictly to verify the true operator if suspicious location or device shifts occur.

💎 Tier 2: The Verified Merchant (Activity & Longevity)

As users spend more time on the platform and consistently complete daily tasks cleanly, the system monitors:

  • Platform Tenure & Metrics: Automated tracking of how long an account has been active and its compliance rate with platform rules (such as utilizing the Marketplace P2P Escrow correctly). No personal or external data is collected for this; it is purely internal platform activity.

👑 Tier 3: The Sovereign Challenger (Challenge Registration)

When users choose to fill out the form to register for elite ecosystem competitions, such as our high-performance custom workstation challenges:

  • Official Business Documentation: This is the exact time the platform requires the submission of official business registration and corporate verification documents. This information is processed under absolute confidentiality to verify the legitimacy of the entity competing for premium tier assets.

3. Account Protection & Key Recovery Safeguards

Because we do not cooperate with external tracking networks, your account recovery is entirely automated through your pre-saved registration details:

  • Strict Matching: If a security key is lost, the platform triggers a synchronized One-Time Password (OTP) to the registration phone number and registration email simultaneously.
  • No Manual Alterations: The system will never accept secondary or random emails/numbers to receive recovery codes. If a user cannot verify through their original registration nodes, access remains locked to prevent hackers from stealing user funds.

4. Data Retention and Erasure

All information, from registration details to corporate challenge documentation, is heavily encrypted. If a participant decides to close their profile or disconnect from the platform nodes, all associated operational data is permanently purged from our active backend systems in accordance with our secure data-deletion protocols.

5. Updates to This Policy

The NAWI-EMPIRE Administration reserves the right to update this policy to align with new technical integrations or ecosystem expansions. However, our core promise remains unchanged: your data is private, secure, and entirely yours.

For inquiries regarding platform data security, contact the NAWI-EMPIRE Administration through the official internal platform support channels.
