## 3. Foundational Principles

### 3.1 Human Interaction First
Platform value originates solely from active human-to-human or merchant-to-customer participation. The system logic is architected to prioritize intentional user-driven queries over automated or passive engagement workflows.

### 3.2 No Passive Feed Dependency
The ecosystem explicitly and permanently rejects the social media product paradigm. The codebase is structurally prohibited from deploying:
* Infinite-scroll entertainment feeds.
* Algorithmic attention-harvesting mechanics or notification manipulation loops.
* Engagement-maximization feed ranking systems.

### 3.3 Participation Economy
Economic value is generated strictly through verified marketplace trade, professional service bookings, industrial asset creation, and certified task completion. Speculative or inflationary economic mechanics are architected out of the framework.

### 3.4 Creator Ownership
All intellectual property remains the absolute property of the creator. Ownership is protected at the compilation layer by cryptographic provenance registries, rendering original master files traceably safe from unauthorized internal cloning or scraping.

### 3.5 Transparent Commerce
All transaction pathways must retain verifiable records suitable for external auditing, legal reconciliation, and compliance processes. The platform logic isolates user balances from direct manipulation, requiring dual-signature ledger state mutation.
